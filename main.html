Maha Rose Art

I am a creative soul who finds artistic expression as a way to communicate to the world. Since I was a young child I always enjoyed coloring in my coloring books, drawing, and painting, etc. At home my father would draw with me. He encouraged me to be the artist that I am today. I feel that art is all around us in each and everything if we just look around us and “see.” I am happiest when I am creating art whether it’s painting, sketching or using a camera to capture the look and feel of beauty that is all around us.
